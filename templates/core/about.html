About Shot at a Deal

We exist to give talented independent artists a direct, legitimate path to the people who can change their lives.

The music industry has always been about who you know. Shot at a Deal changes that. We connect independent artists in the USA directly with real, working music producers — no gatekeepers, no label politics, no pay-to-play schemes disguised as contests.

We currently serve two genres: Rock and Country. Our Rock submissions are reviewed by a producer from Jon Bon Jovi's team. Our Country submissions go to two established producers with deep roots in the American country music scene.

The $100 submission fee is not a money grab. It is a quality filter. It ensures that every song in our producer's queue represents an artist's best, most serious work — not a rough demo or a throwaway track. It also covers the real costs of bandwidth, storage, and platform operations.

Our Promise

  • Every paid submission is listened to by a real producer.
  • Selected artists receive a direct interview with the producer.
  • We will never sell or share your music without your consent.
  • Your submission status is always visible on your dashboard.
